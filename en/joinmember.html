Who is Aigent Member Club?

Aigent Club is a NFT project with the exclusive e-voucher utility, where holders can use their utility at Aigent platform that provides booking services in the form of e-vouchers through mobile applications. The benefit that Aigent Club members will receive is a 50% discount, up to a maximum of 3,000 baht per voucher from the normal voucher price for at least 1 room/day at Aigent Platform.

There will be 2 rarity ranking
  • Rare - 100 pieces: will receive standard utility, PLUS special big event (Lift-time)
  • Standard - 4900 pieces: will receive standard utility,PLUS special big event (2 years from 1/11/2022-1/1/2025)
  • And a special tier – null – is the tier for special airdrop activity, which can be upgraded into rare or standard. The sale starts on 15 November 2022, on Polygon (MATIC) main net.
